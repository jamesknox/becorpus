--INTRODUCTION -----------------------------------------------------

There are 100 documents in the "core" corpus.  The corpus is
constructed to be broadly representative of the kind of input
processed by the English language BioCaster system.  All documents
contain relevant information. 

90% of the documents are derived from the BioCaster corpus.

15%        ProMed-mail
15%        Reuters
10%        BBC
10%        WHO
5%         CBS
5%         CBC
5%         Vietnam_net
5%         Hindustani Times
5%         "The Nation" (Thailand)
5%         All_africa
5%         xinhua (China)
5%         antara (Indonesia)


A further 10 documents are designed to test specific hypothesis.  Not
all of these documents are derived from the BioCaster Corpus.

2%         Historical cases
2%         Hypothetical cases (e.g. recalled food)
2%         Drug/Medical Breakthroughs
2%         Vaccination campaigns
2%         Business and social domain

--SPECIAL CASES -----------------------------------------------------

There are 10 articles in 5 categories (2 articles in each category).

Historical cases (2 articles  ./special_cases/historical)
=========================================================
Articles that contain mentions of specific historical outbreaks, along
with more current information.

946.xml  h/d   hindustantimes reject    India:Meningitis
[Reports on a minor outbreak, with details of historical meningitis
lsoutbreaks]
985.xml  h/d   Promed          publish  Kenya:Rift_Valley_Fever
[Reports on outbreaks in several countries.  Refers to a historical
outbreak in 1997-98]

Also 174.xml (see Drug/Medical Breakthrough section) for a historical
event.
See 185.xml (ProMED section) for a historical anthrax outbreak in Ghana.

Drug/Medical Breakthroughs (2 articles  ./special_cases/drug)
=============================================================
Articles that contain information about both disease outbreak events
and medical research/drug breakthroughs.   These articles are included
to test the system's ability to distinguish between research and drug
breakthroughs and actual disease outbreak and development information.

54.xml    h/d     CBC      publish     Canada/Hong Kong:SARS
[Identification of the virus behind SARS]
174.xml   h/d     Promed  publish     Netherlands:H7N7
[Bird flu:  Covers many different areas.  Including Historical Flu outbreaks]

Vaccination campaigns (2 articles  ./special_cases/vaccination)
===============================================================
Articles that contain information about both disease outbreak *and*
also a vaccination campaign.  These articles are included in order to
test the system's ability to distinguish between disease outbreak and
vaccination campaign information.

449.xml   h/d     WHO      publish    Guinea:Yellow Fever
[Reports deaths and vaccination campaign for Yellow Fever]
206.xml   h/d     ProMED  publish      Burkino_Faso/Niger:Meningitis      
[Reports death and planned vaccination campaign in Burkino_Faso]


Business and Society  (2 articles ./special_cases/business_and_social)
======================================================================
Articles that have a slightly different slant and are not directly
focused on the health and disease domain.  Instead of the health
domain (and "disease" subdomain), they are drawn from the business and
society sections of the biocaster corpus.

451.xml  business Reuters  publish    Turkey:Bird_Flu
[Reports death from bird flu in Turkey from a business perspective]
155.xml  society  nation   publish    Thailand:"Mystery_Infection"
[Reports on a mystery infection suffered by a Thai actor]
